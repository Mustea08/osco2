What Makes Kiwi Movers Different?

We’re committed to providing a simple, quick, safe, and affordable removals service to customers all over London and the wider Greater London area. It’s what we’ve built our business on, and what we’ll continue to do – mainly because it works.

We can also be pretty ingenious when we need to. There’s nothing we love more than putting our heads together and coming up with something clever in a pinch. We’re a bunch of problem-solving nerds, fueled by a team who comes from all over. This is a huge asset to our business, as with experience in project management, engineering, and construction management to name just a few, it’s easy for us to solve any puzzle that comes our way. Whether you’re dealing with bulky furniture, family heirlooms, or pivoting a couch up the staircase, you can be confident that we’ll keep your things safe and secure every time.

Our wide variety of skills and can-do attitude are a big part of why we’re so confident in our service – but it doesn’t mean we rest on our laurels. We’re always striving to get better, try new systems and evolve how we work to make sure our team offers customers the best service each time. We’re also passionate about making sure that we work in a way that is as sustainable, fair, ethical, and transparent as possible, too. That’s how we get fantastic outcomes for our customers, our team, and the environment.
