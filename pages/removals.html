Removals Service Throughout London

From man and van to office relocation, we do it all.

Kiwi Movers provides a full range of professional removals services throughout London.

Whether you’re a family who are moving home or a business looking to relocate to a new office, our friendly, experienced and reliable team are here to make your move go as smooth, swift and stress-free as possible.

Our wide range of removals services includes everything from a full relocation, where we handle all the packing, moving and unloading for you, to a man and van service, for when you just need a bigger vehicle and an extra pair of hands. We also offer storage, shipping and end-of-tenancy cleaning.

We’re a fit, friendly and hard-working bunch at Kiwi Movers, and we pride ourselves on providing an efficient, reliable and high-quality service at competitive prices. Not only will you have peace of mind knowing that your move is being handled by professionals, but you can also rest assured that you’re getting the best value for your money.

What makes us really special (aside from the majority of our staff being Kiwis, of course!) is our problem-solving mentality and unique set of skills and experiences. Staying true to New Zealand’s “Number 8 wire attitude,” our team draw from a variety of industries and disciplines — including project management, engineering and construction management — enabling them to come up with creative solutions to overcome any removals-related challenge.
